Imperial Treasure Hunt

Welcome, noble seekers! To prove your valor in the eyes of the Senate and People of Rome, embark upon this grand quest. Decode the riddles, follow the ancient pathways, and seize the glory that awaits.

  1. Assemble Your Cohort: Form legions of up to four comrades. Choose a legion name worthy of legend.
  2. Obtain the First Scroll: Gather at the Forum’s Eastern Gate (Porta Praetoria) by dawn to receive your inaugural clue.
  3. Decipher the Latin Riddle: Translate the verse inscribed upon the scroll using wit and scholarly skill.
  4. Pursue the Hidden Tablets: Journey to each marked temple in sequence. At each site, discover a tablet bearing the next inscription.
  5. Complete the Trials: Face challenges of strength, cunning, and rhetoric at the Arena of Mars.
  6. Claim the Imperial Prize: Your final destination lies beneath the Arch of Triumph—unearth the chest to claim your reward and honor.

Bonus Challenge

For those seeking additional renown, solve the Emperor’s Cipher hidden within the mosaic border. A secret motto reveals itself only to the keen of eye.

May Jupiter grant you wisdom, and Mars bestow upon you courage. Only the worthy shall bask in the eternal glory of Rome!

FAC ROMAM MAGNUM ITERUM
