A Plea from Gaeius Amellius Varro

To my esteemed friend,

I write to you with a heart full of gratitude. Your tireless efforts in locating my beloved daughter have been nothing short of miraculous. Words cannot express the depth of my appreciation for your unwavering dedication and the risks you undertook on my behalf.

Thanks to you, our family is whole once more. The joy that has returned to our home is a testament to your kindness and bravery. I shall forever be in your debt.

Here is your reward:

As a further token of my gratitude, I have secured tickets to the upcoming chariot race at the Circus Maximus, featuring the renowned Maximus Verstappenus. May the thrill of the competition bring you great pleasure.

  • Tickets for Maximus Verstappenus Chariot Race

With deepest gratitude,

Gaeius Amellius Varro
