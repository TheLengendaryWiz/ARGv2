✠ LVCILLA OF CAPVA ✠

Lucilla of Capua, noble daughter of the esteemed merchant Gaius Aemilius Varro, has gone missing from the streets near the Grand Manor.

She was last seen at dusk, wearing a stola of violet hem and bearing the signet of her house. Lucilla is graceful in stature, of gentle voice, and revered across Capua and Roma alike.

House Varro has declared her disappearance to be of grave concern and calls upon any loyal citizen to aid in her recovery.

A reward, worth more than coin, shall be granted to any who return her safely or offer true word of her fate.

Report to the Praetor or leave discreet word beneath this.
